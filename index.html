About

Project I is a philosophical channel dedicated to the pursuit of truth. Rooted in the Western intellectual tradition, the channel explores questions of metaphysics, ethics, epistemology, and the philosophy of religion.

While grounded in the thought of Catholic Christianity and the scholastic tradition, Project I engages broadly with the history of philosophy — from the ancient Greeks to contemporary debates — seeking dialogue rather than mere argument.

The aim is simple: to think clearly, to reason carefully, and to follow the argument wherever it leads.
